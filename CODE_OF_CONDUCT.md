# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions and code reviews
- Focus on the technical merits of contributions
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Prioritize the community's best interests

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Trolling or deliberately inflammatory comments
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces: GitHub issues, discussions, pull requests, and any public channel representing the project.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All reports will be reviewed and may result in a response deemed appropriate to the circumstances, including temporary or permanent bans from the project.
